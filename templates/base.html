Programming Contest.
  1. Get registered by a CompSocSSC staff member
  2. Visit a question. Solve it
  3. Upload your source code. Select your language
  4. Have a look at your submissions. Traceback will be provided where possible.
  5. Move on to another question when a submission has been termed True. Otherwise keep trying
  6. Solve as many as you can
In case of ties a speed tiebreaker will be held. You will be given a well known algorithm/program and will be asked to implement it in the language of your choice. The first to have a successfully running code will win.
Tiebreakers will only happen if the first place is tied.

Ranking

Ranking is based on how many questions have been solved.

Winner

First to complete all questions wins. In case of time running out first in rank wins. Total time alotted is announced by the administrators
